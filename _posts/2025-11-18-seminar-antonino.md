Antonino Midiri is giving an online seminar at the [Seminar on the Interface of Particle Physics and Gravitational Waves](https://indico.koza.if.uj.edu.pl/event/22/) on ***Gravitational waves from fluid perturbations in first-order phase transitions*** based on on-going work to be published soon related to the production of GWs in first-order phase transitions.
